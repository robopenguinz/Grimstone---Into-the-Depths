# Grimstone: Into The Depths

## Description
In this game, you play as an ancient warrior who has risen from a 1000 year slumber. Your goal is to bring peace back into a cursed world that lies in the dark dungeon of Grimstone.

## Game Features

- **Tutorial System**: Interactive tutorial for how to play with checklist task to guide new players through basic controls
- **Combat System**: Click to attack combat with smooth animations
- **Dynamic Camera**: Smooth following camera that keeps the player centered
- **Inventory System**: Press 'I' to open and manage your inventory
- **Enemy Action**: Skeletons with health systems, damage reaction, and death animations

## Controls
- **Left Mouse Click**: Move character / Attack enemies
- **I Key**: Open/Close inventory
- **Mouse**: Navigate UI and menus

## Combat System
- Attack range: 3 units
- Attack damage: 25 HP per hit
- Enemy health: 100 HP total
- Damage timing: 0.5 seconds into attack animation
